In this folder:

change_percent_year.jpg: Plot of tax changes each year, with the percent change on the y axis. States with no change are excluded. The size of the point corresponds to the number of changes of that size in that year.

inflation_changes.txt: Regression results of regressing state-level inflation on tax changes. More description in the file.

nbr_changes_yr.jpg: Plot of the number of state-level tax changes per year

rates_allyears.jpg: Histogram of all state sales tax rates, pooling all years

rates_national.jpg: Sales tax rates per year averaged across all states (all states weighted equally)

rates_state.jpg: Sales tax rates per year for each state

rates_state_changedonly.jpg: Sales tax rates per year for each state, only showing states that had at least one change in the tax rate.
